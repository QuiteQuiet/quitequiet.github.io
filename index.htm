How to:

  1. Select the tier you wish to play.
  2. Generate a new full team by clicking the button.
  3. For any Pokemon that faint, click that Pokemon's name to mark it as fainted.
  4. If you won, click the refill button, otherwise reload the page to start a new attempt.
  5. The refill button will automatically roll as many Pokemon as you marked as fainted, and fill in the missing spots on the team. No care is taken to team synergy at this stage, however.
